🏛️ Congressional App Challenge 2025

AidSense

Essential resources for those in need

A mobile and web application connecting underprivileged individuals in New Jersey's 9th Congressional District with vital community resources through an intelligent AI-powered chat interface.

🤖

AI-Powered Search

Natural language chat to find what you need - just ask "I need food" or "where can I get help?"

🍽️

Essential Services

Food banks, shelters, healthcare, free WiFi, clothing, and emergency services

📍

Location-Based

Find the closest resources to you with real-time distance and directions

📱

Works Offline

Access saved locations and basic search even without internet connection
